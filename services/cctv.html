◆ What we deliver

Capabilities.

Every capability below is delivered in-house by our own engineers — surveyed, installed, commissioned and maintained against the standards that matter.

01↗

IP CCTV

Full IP infrastructures — fixed-dome, bullet, multisensor and PTZ — over PoE with network-grade switching and UPS.

02↗

ANPR

Automatic number-plate recognition for car parks, gate triggers, access vetting and allow-list / deny-list workflows.

03↗

Temperature screening

Thermal imaging cameras for perimeter intrusion, fire risk detection and entry screening applications.

04↗

IP analytics

Line crossing, loitering, object left / removed and people-count analytics — edge-based and VMS-integrated.

05↗

Remote monitoring

Cloud-managed VMS with role-based access, remote playback, encrypted streaming and multi-site dashboards.

06↗

Storage & retention

On-prem NVR, hybrid and cloud storage — engineered around your GDPR retention policy and bitrate needs.
