◆ Aspirating

Very early warning smoke detection — before it becomes a fire.

An aspiration detection system is a highly sensitive form of fire detection that can identify a fire at its earliest stage. Air is drawn by a pump from multiple sample points, then tested continuously for the presence of smoke. VoltSecure designs, installs, commissions and maintains VESDA aspirating detection.
