◆ Intruder

Graded intruder alarm systems — monitored, certified and police-response ready.

Grade 2 and Grade 3 intruder systems for commercial, retail and residential sites — installed and maintained to PD 6662:2017 and BS EN 50131. Optional URN-backed police response and fully app-monitored.

SSAIB PD 6662:2017 BS EN 50131-1 Grade 2 / 3
◆ What we deliver

Capabilities.

Every capability below is delivered in-house by our own engineers — surveyed, installed, commissioned and maintained against the standards that matter.

01↗

Graded alarm systems

Grade 2 for light-commercial premises; Grade 3 for higher-risk commercial and cash-handling sites — designed to the installed risk.

02↗

Monitored alarm systems

24/7 ARC (Alarm Receiving Centre) signalling over IP and dual-path, with URN-backed police response and keyholder escalation.

03↗

App monitored systems

Push-notification app control — arm / disarm, zone status, event log and camera verification from any device.

04↗

Confirmed signalling

BS 8243 confirmed alarms reduce false-alarm activations and maintain Level 1 police response.

05↗

Intelligent zoning

Perimeter, part-set, night-set and custom zones — tailored to how the building is actually used.

06↗

Takeover & upgrade

Panel swap-outs, legacy-to-IP migrations and upgrades to hybrid wired / wireless systems.

◆ How we deliver

A four-step delivery process.

Step 01

Risk grading

Assess site risk and determine the correct security grade.

Step 02

Design

Panel, detection, signalling path and keyholder plan.

Step 03

Install

SSAIB-approved engineers, certificated cabling and commissioning.

Step 04

Monitor

Connect to ARC, register URN, handover with full documentation.
