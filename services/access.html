◆ Access

Who, when and where - access control at the forefront.

Access control should be at the forefront of every commercial property. Knowing who, when and where staff and visitors move across your premises is critical for both security and health & safety - and we design systems that make it effortless.

BS EN 60839-11 Biometric Mobile credential Multi-site
◆ What we deliver

Capabilities.

Every capability below is delivered in-house by our own engineers - surveyed, installed, commissioned and maintained against the standards that matter.

01↗

Facial recognition

Non-contact facial recognition for reception, turnstiles and restricted areas - GDPR-compliant.

02↗

Biometric

Fingerprint and vein-reader access for high-security zones - fast, accurate, tail-gate resistant.

03↗

Proximity card

HID, MIFARE and DESFire EV3 card systems - single-site or federated across estates.

04↗

Managed access systems

Cloud or on-prem software with role-based access, audit trails and visitor workflows.

05↗

Thermal recognition

Thermal entry screening for perimeter and cleanroom applications.

06↗

Barrier access

Turnstiles, speed-gates and pedestrian barriers integrated with access control and CCTV.

07↗

Gate access

Vehicle gates with ANPR, intercom and remote unlock - escalation to keyholder on force.

08↗

Mobile app access

Smartphone credentials via Bluetooth / NFC - no cards to lose, no fobs to replace.

◆ How we deliver

A four-step delivery process.

Step 01

Survey

Door schedule, user groups, visitor flow and integrations.

Step 02

Design

Reader & credential choice, software platform, interlock plan.

Step 03

Install

Secure cabling, locks, PSUs, readers and software config.

Step 04

Go-live

User / card enrolment, training and handover documentation.
