◆ Data

Structured cabling, data and networking — installed for the lifespan.

From wireless point-to-point links to complete IP infrastructures, VoltSecure will design, install and commission every data project with the utmost care, communication and — most importantly — quality.

Cat 6A OM4 / OS2 BICSI aligned 25-yr warranty
◆ What we deliver

Capabilities.

Every capability below is delivered in-house by our own engineers — surveyed, installed, commissioned and maintained against the standards that matter.

01↗

Structured cabling

Cat 6 / 6A / 7 copper and OM4 / OS2 fibre backbones — tested, certified and labelled for handover.

02↗

Fibre backbones

Single-mode and multi-mode fibre trunks for campus, multi-building and multi-floor deployments.

03↗

Comms cabinets

19-inch rack supply, install and patching — managed cooling, UPS and power distribution.

04↗

Wireless P2P

Point-to-point and point-to-multipoint wireless bridges for temporary sites, CCTV and campus links.

05↗

IP infrastructure

PoE switching, VLAN design, managed access points and firewall integration for IP-converged installations.

06↗

Certification & docs

Fluke-tested link certification, as-built schematics and O&M packs delivered with every handover.

A four-step delivery process.

Step 01

Design

Cable schedule, containment routes, cabinet and power plan.

Step 02

Install

Clean installation, labelled terminations, dressed cabinets.

Step 03

Test

Fluke-certified to Cat 6A / OM4 standards, full link reports.

Step 04

Handover

Documentation pack, as-built drawings and warranty register.
