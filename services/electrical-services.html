◆ What we deliver

Capabilities.

Every capability below is delivered in-house by our own engineers — surveyed, installed, commissioned and maintained against the standards that matter.

01↗

Mains power distribution

LV distribution, metering, load balancing and power-factor correction for commercial and light-industrial estates.

02↗

Electrical installation

New-build and refurbishment electrical installation — containment, cabling, final circuits, lighting and power.

03↗

Electrical testing

Periodic inspection & testing (EICR), portable-appliance testing and pre-handover commissioning certificates.

04↗

Fault verification & repair

Reactive diagnosis, fault-finding and repair across commercial distribution, lighting and small-power circuits.

05↗

Cable management

Containment design and install — tray, basket, trunking and conduit — coordinated with BMS, data and fire services.

06↗

Emergency lighting

BS 5266 compliant design, install and six-monthly functional / annual duration testing with full certification.

07↗

EV smart charging

OZEV-approved EV charge-point installation — single sockets to destination chargers with load management and OCPP integration.

08↗

Smart home solutions

Scene lighting, automated blinds, heating control and integrated dashboards for residential and high-end estates.
