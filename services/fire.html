◆ Fire

Fire detection & alarm systems — BAFE SP203-1 across every module.

VoltSecure is an industry-leading fire protection system supplier. Conventional, addressable and wireless — designed, installed, commissioned and maintained to BS 5839-1 and BS 5839-6 by BAFE-certified engineers.

◆ What we deliver

Capabilities.

Every capability below is delivered in-house by our own engineers — surveyed, installed, commissioned and maintained against the standards that matter.

01↗

Conventional

Zone-based systems for small to medium premises — cost-effective and compliant for lower-complexity sites.

02↗

Addressable

Point-identified loops for commercial and multi-occupancy buildings — faster fault location, easier maintenance.

03↗

Conventional addressable

Hybrid systems that combine zone simplicity with point-level diagnostics on critical areas.

04↗

Wireless

Radio-based detection for heritage sites, fast-track fit-outs and environments where cabling is impractical.

05↗

Aspirating detection

Very early warning smoke detection for data centres, plant rooms and high-value environments.

06↗

Emergency voice comms

BS 5839-9 disabled refuge systems and emergency voice communication for high-rise and public venues.

◆ How we deliver

A four-step delivery process.

Step 01

Design

Category (L1–M, P1, etc.), panel and device plan, cause & effect.

Step 02

Install

Certified cabling, device addressing and ingress protection.

Step 03

Commission

Panel config, cause & effect testing, end-user training.

Step 04

Maintain

Six-monthly and annual service visits, BS 5839 compliant logbook.
