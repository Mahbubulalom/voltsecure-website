Scope

Caerphilly CBC's Foxes Lane office required a full lighting replacement programme — dated fluorescent luminaires swapped out for modern LED panels with scene programming, occupancy sensing and daylight harvesting.

What we delivered

  • Replacement LED panel luminaires throughout open-plan and meeting rooms
  • DALI-enabled occupancy sensors and scene programming
  • Daylight harvesting on perimeter zones
  • Emergency lighting tests and certification to BS 5266
  • Commissioning, end-user training and maintenance handover
