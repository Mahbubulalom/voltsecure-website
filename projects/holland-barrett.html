Scope

VoltSecure supported Holland & Barrett with a national rollout programme to create treatment rooms within existing live stores across multiple locations. The works covered the full electrical package associated with the treatment room installations, including power, lighting, emergency lighting, data and fire alarm alterations.

Because the works were carried out within live retail stores, disruption had to be kept to an absolute minimum - safety, cleanliness, noise control, working around store teams and protecting the customer experience were all key considerations throughout.

What we delivered

  • Electrical works for treatment room installations
  • Lighting installation and alterations
  • Emergency lighting works
  • Data cabling and associated connectivity
  • Fire alarm alterations and integration works
  • Works within live retail stores
  • Testing, commissioning and handover documentation
