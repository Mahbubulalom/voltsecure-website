Scope

VoltSecure delivered electrical works across a series of Lloyds Bank refurbishment projects throughout Wales and the West, supporting the upgrade and improvement of existing banking environments. The projects involved all electrical aspects associated with the refurbishments, requiring careful planning, professional site delivery and a clear understanding of working within sensitive commercial premises.

Across multiple locations, VoltSecure had to maintain a consistent standard while adapting to different site layouts, access arrangements and project requirements.

What we delivered

  • Electrical refurbishment works across multiple Lloyds Bank sites
  • Power and containment installation
  • Lighting installation and alterations
  • Small power and final circuit works
  • Coordination with refurbishment teams and other trades
  • Testing, commissioning and certification
  • Handover documentation on completion
