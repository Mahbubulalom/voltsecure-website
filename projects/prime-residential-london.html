Scope

A prime central London residence required a full electrical rewire during a gut-refurbishment. Scope covered every lighting circuit, bespoke accessories, scene-programmed KNX control, Lutron shading, AV distribution, a plant room refresh and EV charging provision at garage level.

Work was coordinated with the interior designer and architect — every terminal, switch plate and accessory had to land on a millimetre grid.

What we delivered

  • Full electrical rewire to BS 7671 (18th Edition Amd 2) with NICEIC certification
  • KNX scene lighting across every principal room and external zones
  • Lutron shading and blind control — tied to scenes and solar-tracking
  • Multi-zone AV distribution with in-ceiling and in-wall speakers
  • Dedicated circuits for bespoke appliances, wine room and sauna plant
  • EV charge point at garage level with load-balancing
  • Emergency lighting, interlinked smoke / heat / CO detection

Why it mattered

Prime residential is a precision trade. Clients at this level expect zero visible compromise — no unexpected exposed runs, no unsightly accessories, scenes that work as described. Every installation was coordinated with the architectural drawing set and every deviation signed off by the principal designer.
