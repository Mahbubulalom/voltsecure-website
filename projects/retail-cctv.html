Scope

VoltSecure was brought in via the principal contractor to deliver IP CCTV installations for a global fashion retail chain's stores across the UK and continental Europe. Every store is scoped, surveyed, installed and commissioned to SSAIB-certified standards — consistent kit list, consistent cable dressing, consistent documentation pack.

Staffing levels have more than doubled year on year to support this rollout, with FESS apprentices trained on the programme.

What we delivered

  • IP CCTV across all store zones — shop floor, stockroom, cash offices, service yards
  • PoE switching, VMS, NVR storage and cloud-accessible remote playback
  • IP analytics — line crossing, loitering, people-count and object-left detection
  • Structured Cat 6A data cabling to comms cabinet, Fluke-certified on handover
  • GDPR-compliant retention, signage and DPIA support documentation
  • Consistent as-built drawings and O&M pack delivered per store

Geographic coverage

Installations have spanned the UK and multiple European countries — each site surveyed, installed and commissioned by our own engineers. The rollout runs continuously alongside our domestic project workload and underpins our SSAIB and BS EN 62676 certification.
