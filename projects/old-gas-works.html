The brief.

A temporary modular housing scheme on the site of the old Grangetown gasworks — 48 relocatable homes across three apartment blocks and an adjoining office block. The client needed a single accredited electrical contractor who could handle the full scope: containment, power, lighting, certification, and the external site infrastructure.

What we delivered.

We took the scheme end-to-end. First and second fix across all communal areas, full inspection and testing of every apartment, and all external and street lighting on the development. EV charging points were installed on the resident bays at the same time, avoiding the usual retrofit cost.

  • 1st & 2nd fix across all communal areas of the three blocks and office.
  • Inspection & Test of every apartment — full electrical certification handed over on completion.
  • Communal and external lighting, including street-lighting infrastructure across the site.
  • EV charging stations installed on resident parking bays.
