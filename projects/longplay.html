The brief.

An independent coffee brand opening their first flagship needed the full electrical scope handled by a single accredited contractor — and then a maintenance partner who could keep the site trading through peak hours without drama. Because it's a hospitality fit-out, the electrical installation had to coordinate with the mechanical trades, the espresso suppliers and the shopfitters to a tight pre-opening programme.

What we delivered.

  • Full electrical fit-out — power distribution, feature and task lighting, equipment circuits for espresso machines and grinders.
  • Data infrastructure — EPoS cabling and comms for front-of-house operations.
  • Commissioning & certification to BS 7671 with a full NICEIC certificate handed to the client at sign-off.
  • Ongoing PPM — scheduled planned-preventative maintenance covering distribution boards, emergency lighting and equipment circuits.
