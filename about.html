◆ Founded 2020

South Wales-born. UK and Europe-operating. Engineer-led from day one.

The short version.

VoltSecure was founded by Cai, a CTSP-certified (Certified Technical Security Professional) engineer who started in the fire & security industry in 2006 as an apprentice in South Wales, and his business partner Terry Jones. Between them they brought nearly two decades of fire, security, biometric and cyber-security experience into one accredited company.

The business started on the back end of the pandemic in 2020. Within its first year it had passed NICEIC, SSAIB and Constructionline audits — the hardest three to get through as a new business — and landed a national electrical contract on the strength of that paperwork. ECA, FSA and JIB memberships followed, along with a BAFE SP203-1 certification covering all four modules of fire detection and alarm work.

Today, staffing levels have more than doubled year on year. We run electrical and FESS apprenticeships, supply integrated CCTV for a well-known high-street retailer across the UK and continental Europe, and have been shortlisted by The Great British Entrepreneur Awards (Start-Up Entrepreneur of the Year, Wales) and the FSM Awards 2026.

◆ How we operate

Four commitments
we make on every job.

01

Designed to standards

Every project delivered against the relevant British or European Standard — BS 7671, BS 5839, BS EN 62676, PD 6662, BS EN 60839. No shortcuts, no exceptions.

02

Audited & assured

Independent UKAS-accredited bodies audit our technical work, our documentation and our live jobs on an ongoing surveillance basis. Our certificates mean something.

03

Invested in people

JIB-graded workforce, ECS-carded operatives, funded apprenticeships and structured CPD. Fair pay, welfare benefits and pension cover as the baseline.

04

Socially engaged

UK supply chain, EV fleet, community sponsorships and local employment. Social value baked in — not bolted on to tick a box.
