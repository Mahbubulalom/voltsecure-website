What we deliver on the ground.

The same nine disciplines we run in the UK - sequenced for cross-border framework agreements, IS 3218 fire-system referencing where it applies, and Irish-market insurance compliance.

Fire

Fire detection & alarms

Conventional, addressable and aspirating systems to BS 5839 / IS 3218 - design, install, commission and maintain.

Security

Security & CCTV

IP CCTV, intruder and access control to BS EN 62676 / EN 50131 with SSAIB-grade design and commissioning.

Electrical

Electrical installation

HV/LV distribution, sub-mains, small-power and lighting design - NICEIC certified, working to BS 7671 and Irish ETCI rules where required.

Maintenance

Maintenance contracts

Quarterly maintenance, 24/7 callout on live contracts and full insurer-ready documentation packs.
