Why VoltSecure.

We're an engineer-led company. Founded in 2020 by an apprentice who came up through the trade, scaled to a UK-and-Europe operation in five years, and still doing the work properly - to the standards that matter, with the certifications to back it up.

If you want to learn fire and security from the people designing and commissioning the systems - rather than reselling someone else's - this is the place.

What we offer

  • JIB-graded pay. Transparent rates aligned to the Joint Industry Board scale.
  • Funded apprenticeships. Electrical installation and FESS pathways, fully funded with day-release at a partner college.
  • Manufacturer training. HikVision, Paxton, Texecom, Advanced and more - paid for by the company, sat for in working hours.
  • Modern fleet. Marked company vans, EV transition under way, full tool kit issued.
  • Pension & sick pay above statutory. Confirmed in your offer.
  • Real career path. Apprentice → engineer → senior engineer → project manager. We're growing - the headroom is genuine, not a slogan.

How to apply

Send your CV and a short note about which role to admin@voltsecure.co.uk. We read every application and reply within five working days. No agencies, please.

What to expect from the process

  • An informal phone call with a member of the team to talk through experience and what you're after.
  • A site or office visit so you can see how we operate - and ask anything we haven't covered.
  • A formal offer including grade, pay, holiday and pension.
  • A two-week induction with a senior engineer mentor before you're issued your van and tools.

Equal opportunities

We hire on the basis of skill, attitude and fit - nothing else. VoltSecure is committed to providing an inclusive workplace and welcomes applications from underrepresented groups in the trades. Our recruitment, pay and progression decisions are reviewed against this commitment annually.
